Marketing and public relations

The team has been taking Marketing to the next level via innovative strategies and channels thanks to the combination of 3 sub departments within the same department: - Community management which is responsible for the content writing and managing social media and other channels. - digital marketing management to reassure creative new strategies and to increase brand recognition in the society. - customer experience management to provide a market analysis in order to understand our target, to create a follow up survey and to track our EPs making sure that they are living the best experience of their lives.

ICPoland

August 2016

Bringing young leaders from around the world together to impact and act upon the most important global issues, the International Conference made its 68th edition and took place in Poland. More than 120 Countries and territories were present, including Tunisia. Representing both AIESEC in Tunisia and Lc Hadrumet, our Lcp was lucky enough to join other Tunisian leaders and enjoy a 10 day amazing journey.I'm sure you're all excited to what happened there because I am. starting by what one of the MCPs said, Mansour writes " « Our main purpose in AIESEC is Leadership Development » and this is what I witnessed in IC Poland. It all started from the opening ceremony which was amazing. An alumnus shared such an emotional experience where despite of the accident he had,he decided to not to give up had officially started his startup. moreover, It's during this IC where I really saw the contribution of Alumni. There were PAI of the term 1980/1981 and a lot of old AIESECers standing in the hall of fame. I also noticed how the technical part of AIESEC was so interesting and I can say now that AIESEC is indeed accessible to everyone and everywhere. The output of #ICPoland was all about Leadership development;and that in AIESEC, quality is more important than quantity. "
